Coqui Web App

Analyzing audio input from your Coqui

Note: you'll need to allow this web page to access your microphone. When the web page loads, please select 'share' or 'allow'. For troubleshooting tips, go here.

Above, you're seeing a histogram of all of the frequencies that are coming in through your microphone jack. The dominant frequency is marked with a yellow bar; and we're displaying that dominant frequency for you, in Hertz. Typically, this dominant frequency is the data that corresponds to your sensor signal.

Geolocation

Often, a Coqui is used to analyze materials that came from a particular place. In the above map, select the location where you gathered your sample, and then click 'mark location', below.
